Word Solitaire

How To Play

"A little luck, a little skill, a lotta fun"

You have 7 letter tiles in your hand, and 43 in the bag. Make valid scrabble words out of your hand by clicking the letters and submitting the word for points. (2-letter minimum words).

When you play a word your hand will be replenished with tiles from the bag. Your goal is to use up ALL 50 letter tiles with the highest point total possible.

Points:

  • 2-letters: 2pts
  • 3-letters: 3pts
  • 4-letters: 5pts
  • 5-letters: 7pts
  • 6-letters: 8pts
  • All 7 letters: 15pts

Tips:

  1. Keep an eye on your 'Tiles in bag' count to plan your end game strategy with the final few letters. Your goal is to use them all up.
  2. Use up difficult letters early (J, Q, X, Z) so you don't get stuck with them at the end.
  3. If you get stuck, use the Deal New Hand button to get 7 new random letters from the bag.
  4. Don't forget about 2-letter words, they can come in handy in a pinch!

Good luck!
